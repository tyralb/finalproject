Introduction

Reading has been one of my favorite things to do for as long as I can remember. Reading is an important practice for me since it allows me to gain knowledge and discover new information. I also enjoy reading just because I think it's fun, and it always offers a nice distraction from real life events.

What I Like to Read

Non-Fiction

  • Biographies: retelling a life story
  • Historical nonfiction: recounting a historical event or specific time period
  • Books that critically examine important issues such as race, gender, and more

Fiction

  • Literary Fiction
  • Historical Fiction
  • Books that will make me cry
